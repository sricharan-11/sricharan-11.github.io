Architecture

DOs

  • Design for Failure: Always assume components will fail and build resilience.
  • Keep it Simple: Complexity is the enemy of reliability.
  • Document Decisions: ADRs (Architecture Decision Records) are your best friends.
  • Automate Everything: From testing to deployment, manual processes are bugs waiting to happen.

DON'Ts

  • Don't Optimize Prematurely: Measure first, then optimize where it matters.
  • Don't Ignore Security: Security is not an afterthought; it's a foundational requirement.
  • Don't Reinvent the Wheel: Use established patterns and libraries unless you have a very specific reason not to.
  • Don't Couple Tightly: Loose coupling allows for independent evolution of services.
